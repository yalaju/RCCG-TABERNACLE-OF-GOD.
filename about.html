TABERNACLE OF GOD

WHO WE ARE !!

This is all you need to know about Redeemed Christian Church of God, TABERNACLE OF GOD. The Parish was established in June 2008, by the erstwhile Pastor in charge of the Parish, Pastor Mathew Amailo. The church moved location several times, till God made room for the current Church Auditorium at 48, Apapa road, Ebute Metta, Lagos. Directly adjacent to the Church auditorium (no 50 Apapa road) is the Church office building, which houses the Church administrative office and Pastors/ ministers offices, the Children and Youth classes, Technical/ Sound department and other spaces which are in active use.

The current Pastor in charge of the Parish is Assistant Pastor ‘Tunde Sanni. The parish is also the Headquarters for Area 006 of Zone 06 under Lagos Province 48 of the Redeemed Christian Church of God. He is ably assisted by Assistant Pastor ‘Toyin Akanle and several faithful ministers in the lord’s vineyard.

The church is composed of various smaller groups, including the Children’s & Teens church, The Youth church and various other departments required for the efficient running of services and church programs, and for the dissemination of the Gospel of our Lord Jesus Christ. The church throughout its existence has always made an impact in the immediate environment, whilst maintaining adherence to the doctrines and beliefs of the parent church. To achieve this goal.

Mission and Vision

What are we about at Tabernacle of God? We aim to raise men and women of God, adept in the knowledge of God, empowered by the Holy Spirit to go into World and affect it positively, thereby aiding in the establishment of God’s Kingdom here on earth.

We believe that nobody come into the church by accident, and that everyone who comes into the fellowship at Tabernacle of God has been specifically chosen to receive life, be renewed in their inner Man, and restored to the Glory that God had envisioned for each person.
