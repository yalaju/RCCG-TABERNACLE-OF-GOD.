PASTOR TUNDE SANNI

Area Pastor's Speech

From the Pastorate

You are welcome to RCCG Tabernacle of God(TOG), where God dwells and where we daily experience the goodness and mercy of our God. We feel honored to bear the name of the Lord, and hope you truly experience the love of God as you partake in any of our online or physical meetings.
Our parish was established 14 years ago, as part of the evangelical vision of the Redeemed Christian Church of God(RCCG).We belong to Region 20 of RCCG, otherwise known as the ‘Apapa family’, which prides itself as been on the cutting edge of what God is doing in the Mission and the Ecclesia.
Our parish comprises many youth, who the pastorate encourage in the belief that the Youth represent the future and the army God will use in establishing His purpose on Earth.
Our services are lively, with emphasis on worship and the living word, which we believe is the basis for living and our existence.
As you explore the various activities on offer, we pray that God touches you in a special way and hope that we would have been able to impact your life positively and contribute to you achieving your God given purpose on this side of eternity.
God bless you.

The Pastorate
RCCG Tabernacle of God.
