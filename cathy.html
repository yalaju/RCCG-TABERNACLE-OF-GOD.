PASTORESS CATHY.

Mummy's Speech

“The Lord replied, “My Presence will go with you, and I will give you rest.” Then Moses said to him, “If your Presence does not go with us, do not send us up from here. How will anyone know that you are pleased with me and with your people unless you go with us? What else will distinguish me and your people from all the other people on the face of the earth?””
Exodus 33:14-16 NIV

To know that God is “with you” is the greatest blessing and gift that God offered to His Children on earth, those who have accepted Him as Lord and Saviour. When Jesus was departing from the earth He promised us the Holy Spirit will be with us. He understood that to live victoriously on earth will be impossible without the help of the Holy Spirit (John 14:16).

David knew this in Psalm 23, when he said “Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I shall fear no evil, for YOU ARE WITH ME.” He knew he could boldly face anything – even death – because God was with him.

When we become Christians, The Holy Spirit comes INTO our life, HE IS WITH US, and He is IN US, all the time! No matter where we are or what we’re going through, the King of Kings and Lord of Lord, can NEVER leave us or forsake us. That is the most comforting and encouraging part about being a Believer, and oh what a privilege we enjoy knowing that we not only dwell and rest in His presence all the days of our lives here on earth, but even in eternity. Glory Hallelujah!! Amen.
