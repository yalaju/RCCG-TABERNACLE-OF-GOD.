THE ELECT

WHO WE ARE !!

Teens Church The Elect Teens Church is a fun place that helps teenagers discover themselves in God and how they can impact their community with the love of God. It’s a place of adventure into God.

The teens church is organised to raise excellent Godly teenagers in this generation. The church includes children from thirteen (13) to nineteen (19) years of age. The church teaches based on the word of God, on practical issues that they will undoubtedly have to face in this current generation, so that they are adequately prepared as they begin their transformation to young adults.

The church organizes programs that encourage the teenagers to explore their creative talents within the church; the teens church is also involved in the annual program organised in collaboration with the Youth church and Choir, and various talks such as career talks, relationship talks, moral and discipline talks are also organized to assist in career decisions that will benefit them in future, based on their areas of strength.
